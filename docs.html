üéÆ How to Play

Step 1: Think of a Word

Think of any valid word and click the "Done" button.

Step 2: Answer Questions

The AI will ask you a series of yes/no questions to narrow down the category of your word:

  • First, it asks about the main category (e.g., "Is it living?")
  • If you answer YES, it jumps to subcategories (e.g., "Is it an animal?")
  • If you answer NO, it moves to the next category

Step 3: Guessing Phase

Once the AI has enough information, it starts guessing. You have 3 options:

  • ‚úì Correct! - The AI guessed right. You lose 1 point.
  • ‚úó Wrong - The AI made a mistake. Try again up to 3 times.
  • üí° Need a Hint - Give the 3rd letter of your word. The AI gets 3 more guesses.

Step 4: Give Up (Optional)

If the AI can't guess after all attempts, you can reveal your word and gain 1 point.

There are 7 main categories:

Priority System

The game learns from your answers:

  • YES answer ‚Üí Priority increases for that category
  • NO answer ‚Üí Priority stays the same
  • Next game: Categories with higher priority are asked first

Guessing Logic

  • Words are picked randomly from the selected subcategory
  • Agent gets exactly 3 guesses
  • After 3 failed guesses, ask for the 3rd letter as a hint
  • After hint, agent gets 3 more guesses

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What if the AI guesses a word that doesn't exist?

A: The AI only picks words from the supported words database. If you don't recognize it, check the "Supported Words" page.

Q: Can I play offline?

A: Yes! The game works entirely offline. All data is stored locally in data.json.

Q: Does the game store my personal information?

A: No. The game only stores category priorities and your score in your browser's LocalStorage. No data is sent to any server.

Q: How does the priority system work?

A: When you answer YES to a category question, its priority increases by 1. Next game, highest priority categories are asked first. This helps the AI learn your word patterns.

Q: Why does the hint only ask for the 3rd letter?

A: The 3rd letter is statistically one of the most distinctive positions in 4-letter words, making it an effective hint without being too revealing.

Q: Can I clear my score and learning data?

A: Open browser DevTools (F12) ‚Üí Application ‚Üí LocalStorage ‚Üí Clear the "miniAkinatorPriorities" and "miniAkinatorScore" entries.

Q: What if no words match after I give a hint?

A: This happens if the 3rd letter you provide has no matching words in your selected category. Try a different letter.

Q: How many words are in the game?

A: Over 500 carefully selected words. Check the "Supported Words" page for the exact count.
